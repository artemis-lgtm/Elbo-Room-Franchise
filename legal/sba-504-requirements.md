# SBA 504 Loan Program: Requirements & Application Guide

*Last verified: March 25, 2026*

---

## Program Overview

The SBA 504 loan provides long-term, fixed-rate financing for major assets like real estate. It's the primary financing path for the Elbo Room property acquisition.

### Structure
| Component | Percentage | Source | Terms |
|-----------|-----------|--------|-------|
| Bank Loan | 50% | Commercial lender (Wells Fargo) | Market rate, 25yr |
| SBA Debenture | 40% | Certified Development Company | Below-market fixed, 20-25yr |
| Owner Equity | 10% | Borrower | Cash or equivalent |

### On a $15M Property Purchase
| Component | Amount | Estimated Rate | Monthly Payment |
|-----------|--------|---------------|----------------|
| Bank Loan (50%) | $7.5M | 6.5% | ~$50,600 |
| SBA Debenture (40%) | $6.0M | ~5.0% (fixed) | ~$35,100 |
| Owner Equity (10%) | $1.5M | — | — |
| **Total Monthly** | | | **~$85,700** |

**Note:** Total monthly payment of ~$85,700 is significantly better than a conventional $15M loan at ~$101,000/mo. The SBA component saves ~$15,300/month ($183,600/year).

## Eligibility Requirements

### Business Requirements
- [x] For-profit business operating in the US
- [x] Net worth under $15M
- [ ] Average net income under $5M for prior 2 years (VERIFY)
- [x] Business qualifies as "small" under SBA size standards
- [x] Not a passive investment — active management required
- [x] Business in operation for 2+ years (88 years)

### Property Requirements
- [ ] Owner must occupy 51%+ of existing building
- [ ] Property must be used for business operations (not sublease/investment)
- [ ] Property must be in the US
- [x] Can be used for purchase, renovation, or construction

### Use of Funds
- ✅ Purchase of existing building/land
- ✅ Construction of new facility
- ✅ Renovation/modernization of existing property
- ✅ Purchase of long-life equipment
- ❌ Working capital
- ❌ Inventory
- ❌ Debt consolidation (generally)

## Application Process

### Timeline: 6-12 months

**Month 1-2: Preparation**
- Compile 3 years of tax returns (personal + business)
- Prepare current financial statements
- Business plan with 5-year projections
- Property appraisal (independent, SBA-approved)
- Environmental assessment (Phase I minimum)

**Month 3-4: Lender Engagement**
- Identify SBA 504-participating bank (Wells Fargo is one)
- Identify Certified Development Company (CDC) — the SBA's local partner
- Submit application package to both
- Preliminary credit review

**Month 5-8: Underwriting**
- Detailed financial review
- Property inspection/appraisal finalization
- Environmental clearance
- SBA authorization process
- Legal review of entity structure

**Month 9-12: Closing**
- Final loan documents
- Title work and insurance
- Closing and funding
- First payment typically 30-60 days post-closing

### Documents Required
1. SBA Form 1244 (Application)
2. Personal financial statements (all owners with 20%+ ownership)
3. 3 years business tax returns
4. 3 years personal tax returns
5. Interim financial statements (YTD)
6. Business plan with financial projections
7. Property appraisal (SBA-approved appraiser)
8. Phase I Environmental Assessment
9. Purchase agreement or letter of intent
10. Entity documents (articles of organization, operating agreement)
11. Debt schedule (all existing business debt)
12. Lease agreements (current)

## Key Risks for Elbo Room Application

### Risk 1: Owner-Occupied Requirement
**Issue:** If the property is held by a separate LLC that doesn't operate the bar, SBA may not consider it "owner-occupied."
**Mitigation:** Operating LLC must be the borrower. See entity-structure-options.md for recommended approach.

### Risk 2: Property Valuation
**Issue:** If appraised value is significantly different from purchase price, it affects the loan structure.
**Mitigation:** Get an independent appraisal early (before negotiating purchase price).

### Risk 3: Environmental
**Issue:** Fort Lauderdale beachfront property may have environmental concerns (flood zone, contamination from decades of use).
**Mitigation:** Phase I Environmental early. Budget for Phase II if anything flags.

### Risk 4: Multiple Owners
**Issue:** SBA requires personal guarantees from all owners with 20%+ stake. All family members with ownership will need to personally guarantee.
**Mitigation:** Family alignment conversation must happen before application. Everyone signs or no one does.

### Risk 5: Licensing Revenue Recognition
**Issue:** How SBA views intercompany revenue from the Heritage License program (if structured as separate entity).
**Mitigation:** Work with SBA-experienced lender to structure intercompany flows that strengthen rather than complicate the application.

## Cost of SBA 504 Process

| Item | Estimated Cost |
|------|---------------|
| Application fees | $2,500-$5,000 |
| Independent appraisal | $5,000-$15,000 |
| Phase I Environmental | $2,000-$5,000 |
| Legal (closing) | $10,000-$20,000 |
| SBA guarantee fee | ~1% of debenture ($60,000 on $6M) |
| CDC processing fee | ~1.5% of debenture ($90,000 on $6M) |
| **Total closing costs** | **~$170,000-$195,000** |

*These costs are significant but amortized over a 20-25 year loan. The interest savings vs. conventional financing pay for closing costs within 12-14 months.*
